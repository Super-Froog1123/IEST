Who are we

Community

We are a community of 2000+ Endurance Athletes who loves running, cycling, swimming and hiking. Everyone is welcome to be a curator and lead a local group/ theme group.

Science & Technology

We extensively apply statistical models and data visualizations to track one's training progress. We firmly believe in Run Slow to Run Fast, and prioritize lifelong training continuity over absolute performance. We organize study groups of exercise science.

Enablement

We work with race organizer to improve runner experience, by marking/ marshaling/ CP management. We work with brands to find the market fit for their products. We work with curators to engage with their audience.
